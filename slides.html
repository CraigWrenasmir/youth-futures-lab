Ideas worth

This is a TEDx event, and TEDx is built on a simple belief: a good idea, clearly told, can change how people see things.

Today we're pointing that belief at the city you live in.

The challenge brief

You are consultants
to the council

Your job today: choose one part of city life and design a new idea, improvement, service, space, campaign, or invention that would make Newcastle both smarter and kinder for young people.

The future isn't only something adults build for young people. It's something young people should help imagine, shape, challenge, and explain.

The two words that matter

Smart is not enough.
Kind is not enough.

Smart

Good systems, transport, information, design, decisions. A city that works well.

Kind

Safe, welcoming, creative, inclusive, affordable, accessible, human. A city that cares about people.

A smart city is not just a city with better technology. A smart city should also be kinder, fairer, safer, more creative, and more human.
